DEMAND FUNCTION:
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Q = BaseDemand - β·Price - θ·max(0, Price - CompetitorPrice) + γ·Popularity + ε

WHERE:
  • Q: Historical Demand (units)
  • BaseDemand: [150, 300] units (universal)
  • β: Own-price elasticity (BRAND-DEPENDENT)
  • θ: Competitor penalty coefficient (BRAND-DEPENDENT)
  • γ: Popularity sensitivity [20, 60] (universal)
  • ε: Gaussian noise N(0, 5)
